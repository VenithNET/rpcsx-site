About WiiLink

WiiLink is a revival service for the now-defunct Japanese exclusive Wii channels and media channels. This service allows you to use Wii Room, Digicam Print Channel, Kirby TV Channel, and the Food Channel!

2020-2023 WiiLink Team

We are not associated with Nintendo nor its affiliates. We are also not associated with other services for the Wii.
